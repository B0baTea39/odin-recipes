Carne Asada Tacos Recipe

Description

Carne asada tacos are delicious flank steak, Mexican-inspired street tacos! Just load up your tortilla with juicy steak bites, avocado, onion, cotija cheese, and fresh cilantro.

In this recipe, you will learn how to make carne asada tacos in two simple steps: grilling steak and assembling the tacos. Without further ado, let's get to cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds boneless beef top sirloin, cut into thin bite-size slices
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • crushed red pepper to taste
  • 1 lime
  • 1 (28 ounce) can tomatillos
  • 2 fresh jalapeno peppers, seeded
  • 4 tablespoons canola oil, divided
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can beef broth
  • 12 (6 inch) corn tortillas
  • ½ large onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Grill the Carne Asada: Grill the carne asada until it’s cooked to your preference. Slice it against the grain, then chop it into small pieces.
  2. Assemble the Taco: Use a spoon to mash the avocado and spread a large spoonful or two onto each tortilla. Then, top the avocado with chopped carne asada, a sprinkle of cotija cheese, some diced onion and fresh cilantro. Squeeze fresh lime juice on top.
